# Gray's Anatomy plates — public-domain art for The Creature

Source: Wikimedia Commons, [Category:Gray's Anatomy plates](https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Gray%27s_Anatomy_plates).
From *Anatomy of the Human Body* (Henry Gray, plates by Henry Vandyke Carter et al.), **1918 (20th US
edition)** — **public domain** (published pre-1928; Carter d. 1897). Free to use, including commercially.

Pointed at by Hallie (2026-06-13) for the Creature — "a body made of parts," so anatomical engravings
are exactly the register. Downloaded via `Special:FilePath/<file>`. Labels below are by ACTUAL plate
content (the Commons numbering does NOT match organ name — verified each by eye).

| File | Actually depicts | Look | Good for |
|------|------------------|------|----------|
| `Gray1210.png` | **Neck / throat dissection** — sternocleidomastoid, carotid, facial nerve | two-tone (red artery, yellow nerves) on cream | a "part," Keening/voice cards |
| `Gray1244.png` | **Hip & gluteal region** — sciatic nerve, gluteal arteries | yellow bone-tint, line | a limb-part, assembly |
| `Gray1247.png` | **Whole leg** — sciatic nerve to foot, popliteal/tibial arteries | yellow + red, tall | a full limb (tall card art) |
| `Gray989.png`  | **Abdominal viscera** — liver, stomach, pancreas, omentum | line engraving | body/guts, a mended part |
| `Gray990.png`  | **Two abdominal sagittal sections** — diaphragm, liver, intestines | line engraving, paired | wide card art, "before/after" |
| `Gray945.png`  | **Hair-follicle cross-section** — concentric Huxley/Henle layers | microscopic, mandala-like | texture, a power icon |

`Gray1247.png` (the whole leg, 228×725) is the standout for a tall character/part portrait. The
two-tone plates (1210, 1244, 1247) will read best as cards; the line plates (989, 990) are mood/texture.

Note: these are NOT rigged to card slots yet (no `rig-card.sh` run) — raw reference until Hallie picks
which part goes on which card. The Creature's real look is hers to mint; this is the PD palette to draw from.
